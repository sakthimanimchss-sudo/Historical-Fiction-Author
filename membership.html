Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access member content?

After joining, you'll receive a welcome email with login credentials. You can access all member content through the Member Portal, linked at the top of every page.

Can I upgrade my membership later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade from History Circle to History Scholar or History Master at any time. The price difference will be prorated for the remainder of your membership year.

How do advance reader copies work?

Several months before publication, members receive digital ARCs via email. You're encouraged (but not required) to share your honest reviews on Goodreads and retail sites.

Can I gift a membership?

Yes! Gift memberships make wonderful presents for fellow history lovers. Choose the tier and enter the recipient's email at checkout. They'll receive a welcome message with all the details.

What happens to my signed bookplate?

Gold members receive a personalized, signed bookplate with their welcome package. Additional bookplates can be requested for special occasions.

Is my membership auto-renewing?

Yes, memberships renew annually. You'll receive a reminder email 30 days before renewal, and you can cancel at any time in your account settings.
