The Saxon Chronicles

9th Century England • 4 Books

4.8 (2,500+ reviews)

Set against the backdrop of Viking invasions and the birth of England, this epic series follows the journey of a young Saxon nobleman caught between loyalty to his people and an unexpected destiny. Meticulously researched and emotionally gripping, The Saxon Chronicles brings the Dark Ages to vivid life.

Winner, Historical Novel Prize 2021
"A masterpiece of historical fiction" - The Times
200,000+ copies sold worldwide

The Tudor Intrigue

16th Century England • 3 Books

4.7 (1,800+ reviews)

Step into the dangerous world of Tudor politics, where love and betrayal walk hand in hand. This gripping series follows a young courtier navigating the treacherous waters of Henry VIII's court, where one wrong move could mean the difference between favor and the block.

Finalist, British Book Awards 2022

The Roman Eagle

Roman Britain • 2 Books

4.9 (950+ reviews)

At the edge of the empire, a Roman legion faces its greatest challenge. When a standard bearer discovers a conspiracy that threatens not just his unit but the entire province of Britannia, he must choose between duty to Rome and loyalty to the land he's come to call home.

Winner, Classical Fiction Award 2023
