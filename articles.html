The Lost Libraries of the Viking World

New research reveals the surprising extent of literacy and book culture in Viking-age Scandinavia. Far from the illiterate barbarians of popular imagination, the Norse people had a rich tradition of runic writing, poetry, and perhaps even longer texts on perishable materials that have since been lost to time.
